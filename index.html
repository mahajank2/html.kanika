Amrita Pritam

1919 - 2005

One of the most known female writers in the 20th century

"Amrita pritam born on 31 August 1919 -31 October 2005)She was often referred to as the first poetess of the Punjabi language and creative journey spanned all genres,leaving an enduring legacy."


The Information about Amrita Pritam

  • 1919- Amrita Pritam was born in Gujranwala in Punjab (now in Pakistan) on 31st August, 1919.
  • 1930- After her mother's death in 1930, when she was eleven, Amrita and her father moved to Lahore where she lived until her migration to Delhi in 1947.
  • 1936- Her leanings towards romance can be seen in 'Amrit Lehren' (Immortal Waves).
  • 1947- Amrita and her father moved to Lahore where she lived until her migration to Delhi in 1947.
  • 1956- she won the Sahitya Academy Award.
  • 1960- Amrita Pritam's ended her marriage with Pritam Singh.
  • 1969- She won the Padmashree.
  • 1982- she got the Bharathiya Jnanapit Award
  • 1986- she was nominated to the Rajya Sabha, the upper house of the Indian parliament.
  • 2005- she died in her sleep on 31 October 2005 at the age of 86 in New Delhi, after a long illness.
